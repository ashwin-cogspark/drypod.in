*Hello World*, a clichéd title for the first post.
*Yeah, I know.*

Let the first post be about the blog itself.

This blog is a public experiment where I expose my thoughts and bias for the world to see (rather read) without fearing judgement from others. That doesn't mean this space is gonna be filled with explicit content. Rather, this is where I pour my unfiltered inner thoughts on just about any topic under the sun that interests me. More details on the [About](/about) page.

As some of you might have already noticed, this is an Astro blog. I'm serving this through Cloudflare Workers.

The end-to-end is dependent on free-tier services of GitHub and Cloudflare. Since this is a niche blog, I believe free-tier services would go a long way until I'm compelled to migrate to a paid service.

When I first thought of starting this blog, I was in a dilemma on choosing the platform — should I go the free-tier-services route or the managed-services route?

After contemplating this for more than I should have, I zeroed in on the free-tier services for the above-mentioned reason.

I have recently bought a 3D printer — Bambu Lab A1 Mini Combo with AMS. I've already printed stock toys that came pre-loaded, then Batman and Spider-Man bust figurines, some fidget toys, and some auxiliaries for the printer itself.

That's all I have for now. More in the future!
